PORCELAIN MOD READEME

PURPOSE:
The PORCELAIN mod adds colored "stained clay" and "porcelain" nodes to the game.

FEATURES:
Stained clay is created by mixing stain from the OIL_STAINS mod with clay. Stained clay can
be placed or it can be cooked to create a porcelain node, having a darker and richer color.
